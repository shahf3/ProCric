About ProCric

ProCric is an “all in one” cricket management website that simplifies the management of players, teams, matches, and cricket tournaments.

Key Features:
  • Register and manage user accounts.
  • Create and manage cricket teams, including player roster information.
  • Schedule, record, and manage cricket matches.
  • Organize and administer cricket tournaments.
